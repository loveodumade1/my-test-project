ClearBridge Developed Markets Infrastructure Income Model

The ClearBridge Developed Markets Infrastructure Income Model investment process is designed to provide investors with attractive and stable returns through investing in an actively managed portfolio of developed markets listed infrastructure companies. The Model holds a diverse range of developed markets infrastructure securities across a number of infrastructure subsectors such as gas, electricity and water utilities, toll-roads, airports, rail and communication infrastructure and across geographic regions in developed markets.
