Products

OpenEdge in Australia is the investment manager for a selection of global listed infrastructure funds and investment products, each with different characteristics to suit individual investor needs. Investors in other parts of the world can access global listed infrastructure through Franklin Templeton, who offer various fund structures dependant on your location.
