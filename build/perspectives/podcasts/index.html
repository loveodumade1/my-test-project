25 October 2021: In the latest podcast from the ClearBridge Infrastructure Team, Portfolio Manager Nick Langley discusses the team’s view on inflation and why real assets, and in particular infrastructure, are attractive for their inflation protection characteristics. Some key topics covered in the podcast include:

  • Drivers of core and headline inflation and what markets are focusing on today
  • The Infrastructure’s Team’s view that a spike in inflation will be broadly transitory but ultimately settle structurally higher than what investors have been used to in recent history
  • Why investors should allocate to real assets as inflation protection in portfolios and infrastructure’s unique revenue determination mechanisms that make it especially attractive
  • The potential for climate-related inflation and who bears these costs
  • How inflation impacts the various infrastructure sectors differently and why liquidity is an advantage when navigating this environment
